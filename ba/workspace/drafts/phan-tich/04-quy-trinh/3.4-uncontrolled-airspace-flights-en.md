<td></td>

<td><p>There are 2 cases:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>+ Case 1: At a later stage, reattempt to obtain a slot or access the system to identify an alternative slot with available flight levels;</p>
<p>+ Case 2: if no suitable slot is available, the flight shall be delayed in accordance with the assigned slot time, and the DM shall be informed.</p>
</blockquote></td>

<td></td>

<p><strong>In-flight control and support</strong></p></td>
<td style="text-align: left;"><strong>Assess and update information after departure</strong></td>
<td style="text-align: left;"><strong>After departure</strong></td>
<td></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Traffic assessment and en-route risk evaluation</td>
<td>Continue to monitor VNA flights and regularly assess traffic conditions in the Pakistan and Turkmenistan regions to proactively identify traffic operating on routes similar to VNA flights.</td>
<td style="text-align: left;">After all flights have departed</td>
<td></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td></td>
<td><p>Analyze and predict the potential impact of such traffic on VNA operations</p>
<p>Criteria for identifying potentially conflicting traffic. Traffic shall be considered potentially impactful if it meets one or more of the following conditions:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>+ Aircraft operating in the same direction and within 150NM or 20 minutes of flight time from the VNA aircraft;</p>
<p>+ Aircraft operating at the same level or within one adjacent flight level;</p>
<p>+ Obtain the callsign and verify, via Flightradar24, whether the aircraft has flown the same route through KABUL FIR during its last three flights.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>If the route is similar, such traffic shall be closely monitored.</p></td>
<td style="text-align: left;"></td>
<td></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Crew notification</td>
<td><p>When traffic posing a potential risk is identified, immediately inform the flight crew with the following details:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>+ Callsign;</p>
<p>+ Current flight level;</p>
<p>+ Estimated distance from the VNA aircraft.</p>
</blockquote></td>
<td style="text-align: left;"></td>
<td></td>
</tr>

<td></td>
</tr>

</blockquote></td>
