   5 RELATED READING MATERIAL.

 5.1 Guidance Materials for Pilots.

      1. Leidos Flight Service (the FAA-contracted Flight Service provider/resource):
         https://www.1800wxbrief.com.

          obtain enhanced weather and aeronautical information and services.
