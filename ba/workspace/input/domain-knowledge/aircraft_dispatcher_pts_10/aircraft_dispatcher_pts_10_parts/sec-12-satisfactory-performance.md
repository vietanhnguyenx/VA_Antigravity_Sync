### Satisfactory Performance

Satisfactory performance to meet the requirements for certification is based on the applicant's ability to:

1. Perform the Tasks specified in the Areas of Operation within the approved standards outlined in this test book and the aircraft performance capabilities and limitations.
2. Follow normal, abnormal, and emergency procedures as required by the regulations and company procedures.
3. Demonstrate sound judgment, aeronautical decision-making, and dispatch resource management skills.
4. Apply aeronautical knowledge.

"Satisfactory performance" means that, in the judgment of the examiner, the applicant is able to demonstrate skill and correctly respond to the examiner's questions at least **70 percent** of the time (per 14 CFR part 65, section 65.17(b)).
