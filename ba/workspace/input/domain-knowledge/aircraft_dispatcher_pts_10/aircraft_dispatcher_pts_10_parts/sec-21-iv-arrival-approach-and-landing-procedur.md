### IV. Arrival, Approach, and Landing Procedures

#### Task A: ATC and Air Navigation Procedures

**References:** 14 CFR parts 91 and 121; Operations Specifications, General Operations Manual, AIM; FAA-H-8083-15, FAA-H-8083-16; AC 120-28, AC 120-29, AC 120-57.

**Objective:** To determine the applicant exhibits adequate knowledge of:

1. Area arrivals
2. Transition routes and procedures
3. STAR
4. IAP
5. Precision approach procedures
6. Non-precision approach procedures
7. ATC separation minimums
8. ATC priority handling
